ABOUT

Creative Graphic Designer

In 1996, I landed my first job as a designer while attending St. Petersburg College. I became instantly immersed in design. I applied my skills and knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and QuarkXpress to produce granite golf course signage, residential signs, custom 4-color golf brochures, and scorecards. Other companies I worked for specialized in design, printing, advertising, interactive media, web development, live-event production, video, and custom software.

In 2003, I was a Graphic Designer in the Corporate Marketing and Communications Department of a financial holding company, where I spent seven years supporting the Company and its subsidiary marketing needs.

In Feb 2011, I ventured into the healthcare industry, working as the Lead Designer for a software company that developed Biometric Identification Management Software, and specialized in Healthcare IT Security. My position was a hybrid role. I provided all design support for marketing and front-end development and design for the software development team. The Company was widely successful and was acquired in 2015. I was Senior Designer working remotely from Tampa, and on-site in Massachusetts for five years, until the end of April... when like many others my job was impacted by the Covid-19 Pandemic.

Here I am, excited to see where I end up next. Thanks for taking the time to learn about me, and what I can offer.
