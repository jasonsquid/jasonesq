Photography

Florida nature and wildlife gallery

I have a passion for nature and wildlife photography. Whenever I get an opportunity, I try to walk the local trails and wander through the woods to find creatures to photograph. There's a certain feeling of adrenaline when you stumble upon an alligator or approach coyotes who don't seem to be bothered by your presence. I am captivated by eagles and birds of prey, particularly in flight or when encountering a possible meal. Over the years, I have found outdoor photography is a great way to start my mornings and end my days.
